Nice To Meet You !!!

Thanks for joining the 1 million+ strong "Rate It" community! We started "Rate It" in 2022 for people like us - who live in the city but somehow get stuck in the fraud. Today, We have created a platform for the company in the market for people to express their thoughts about the company.

Purpose of createing "Rate It"?

Today, there are many companies in the market. all companies try to provide their best services and people are loving that service. But somehow some people get problem in their other service. And customer reviews are important for any company's service improvement.

So, "Rate It" provides an environment for people to express the good or bad reviews of any company. And by filtering the reviews out of them, it provides to the particular company. So that the company knows its good and bad thing.

How "Rate It" is useful for people?

"Rate It" allows people to express the good and bad things about any company. So that people know about the good and bad things of the company in the market.

So that people can know about some good company in particular field and stay away from fraud company.

How "Rate It" is useful for companies?

"Raate It" gets good and bad reviews from people. Then in the last "Rate It" prepares the report of the particular company reviews and provides it to the company. So that the company knows its good and bad things and tries to improve its copy and become the best.

Any company can contribute with its competitor company and generate a poll among them, so as to know about the good and bad things between them.
